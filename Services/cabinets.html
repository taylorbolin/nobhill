Cabinet Shop


Our cabinetry shop is located on the street level of our building, a stone's throw from the offices nested above. We have found that this allows our construction and design teams to communicate easily and effectively if questions arise during the design or production phases of a project's build. We produce the shop drawings for each cabinet, encouraging collaboration and attention to detail. Our shop also welcomes you to stop in and view your millwork before it is sent out for finishing - something clients enjoy experiencing before the final installation.


Our shop is able to completely outfit any room requiring built-in casework, from a kitchen to a custom closet. We are also capable of building freestanding case goods and furniture pieces or modifying existing cabinetry. We are not limited to standard factory sizes; we build to fit your space and all of the woodwork that we produce is handcrafted and one-of-a-kind.



Our Capabilites:

  • Custom Built-In or Freestanding Cabinetry and Casegoods
  • Refacing/Retro-fitting of Existing Casework

Typical Project Services:

  • Site Measurement and Assessment
  • Material and Hardware Procurement
  • Custom Finishing, Hardware, and Inserts
  • Site Delivery and Installation
