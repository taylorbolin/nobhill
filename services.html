Construction and Project Management


Each of our projects is handled by a project manager and assigned a full-time foreman. Your project manager is the point of contact between you and the project team. He or she manages the schedules, ordering, sub-contractors, and budget. Your project's foreman makes sure that day-to-day activities are running smoothly at the job site.


We also staff the site with a crew comprised of superintendents, carpenters, and laborers from the Nob Hill Construction team. Regular meetings are hosted as often as on a weekly basis for you and the design team to make sure everyone is on the same page and that any issues or questions can be discussed in a timely manner.
