Nob Hill Construction has been completing high-end remodeling and construction projects in the San Francisco Bay Area since 1985. We maintain a versatile approach by offering a selection of general contracting, interior design, and custom cabinetry services all under one roof. No matter the method, our mission remains steadfast: to create a result that will make you proud long after our crews have left your home.

EXPERIENCE
Our doors have been open for 30 years. That has let us develop the expertise to set realistic expectations and approach projects proactively. Above all we find answers, create solutions, and can roll with the punches that will arise with any construction job.


FLEXIBILITY
We can handle your entire project, or work in tandem with an architecture or design firm of your choosing. Our extensive history of working in many high-rise buildings in addition to single-family homes means that we are as comfortable at street level as we are in the clouds.


TEAM
Rich Muratore liked to say: "People make the project." Our departments are helmed by professionals who each have over a decade of industry experience and our highly-skilled Union foremen, carpenters, and laborers have built careers from their employment with Nob Hill Construction.


NETWORK
We have carefully vetted an index of trusted architects, interior designers, engineers, tradespeople, artists and artisans, and other industry professionals over decades of business. You can trust us to assemble and manage the best team for your project.


WORKMANSHIP
Everyone, from our employees to the sub-contractors we bring over your threshold, takes pride in sweating the details and making sure the job is done right. We understand that what we create will reflect on everyone involved - especially you.


SERVICE
Keeping you informed is important to us, so we schedule regular meetings with you and the project team and send progress pictures as your home evolves. From start to finish, our staff is vigilant about keeping your project on time and on budget.
